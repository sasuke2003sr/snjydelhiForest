Government Tenders

Tender 1: Solar Power Project

Deadline: 2024-07-10

Budget: $10 million

Location: Rural Areas

Adani Enterprises has won the bid for the solar power project aimed at providing renewable energy to rural areas. The project aims to install solar panels with a total capacity of 50 MW.

Tender 2: Highway Construction

Deadline: 2024-08-05

Budget: $50 million

Location: National Highways

Reliance Infrastructure has secured the contract for the construction of a 100 km stretch of highway. The project includes road widening, bridge construction, and safety enhancements.

Tender 3: Metro Rail Expansion

Deadline: 2024-09-20

Budget: $200 million

Location: Urban Areas

L&T Construction has been awarded the tender for the expansion of the metro rail network in urban areas. The project involves the construction of new metro lines and stations to improve public transportation.
